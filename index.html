Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical design project take?

The timeline depends on the scope and complexity of the project. A basic logo design might take 1-2 weeks, while a comprehensive brand identity could take 4-6 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer both project-based pricing and retainer options depending on your needs. Our projects typically range from $500 for basic designs to $5,000+ for comprehensive brand systems. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific requirements.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! While we're based in Kampala, Uganda, we work with clients worldwide. We're experienced in remote collaboration and use various tools to ensure seamless communication regardless of location.

What file formats will I receive?

We provide all final designs in multiple formats suitable for both print and digital use. This typically includes AI, EPS, PDF, JPG, PNG, and SVG files. We also provide guidelines on how to use each file type.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, we include a specific number of revision rounds in our proposals to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result. The exact number depends on the project scope and is detailed in our agreement.

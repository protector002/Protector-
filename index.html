Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical design project take?

The timeline depends on the scope and complexity of the project. A basic logo design might take 1-2 weeks, while a comprehensive brand identity could take 4-6 weeks.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer both project-based pricing and retainer options depending on your needs. Our projects typically range from $500 for basic designs to $5,000+ for comprehensive brand systems.
